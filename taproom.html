Visit Our Taproom

Experience the heart of Killer Sprocket Brewery.

The Killer Sprocket production brewery is located in Boronia, Victoria, east of Melbourne.

Open every Friday and Saturday from 1pm until 8pm and Sunday from 1pm until 6pm, the tap room provides a small bar with take-away sales.

No need to book, you can just turn up. There are plenty of seats and tables available for you to enjoy our unique beers.
